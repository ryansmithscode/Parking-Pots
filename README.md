# BA (Hons) Final Major Project (2026)

A wonderful, and unforgettable end to my BA (Hons) experience. Parking Pots is my one-of-a-kind take on the Final Major Project’s theme. Although, the 3D models were commissioned and development was carried out alone (within just under 3 months) it nevertheless had a great outcome. Most, if not all problems were opposed during development through the utilisation of the knowledge acquired from a plethora of feedback and testing from peers.

Made within Unity 6.3 LTS (6000.3.10f1), as well as presented to Radical Forge’s Stephen Hey and NextGen Skills Academy’s Marcia Deakin it was later successfully published on Google Play.

Assets Utilised: Dynamic Bone
